The part of engineering I care about is where the simulation meets the real world.

I am a penultimate-year Mechanical Engineering (Honours) student at the University of Auckland. Most of my projects start as a brief and a hard constraint, and end as something physical I can measure. I model it in CAD, predict how it behaves with FEA, machine it, and test whether the prediction was right. Alongside that, a workshop internship gave me real hands-on time with tools and fabrication, and my mechatronics work has built up my MATLAB, C, and Arduino skills.
